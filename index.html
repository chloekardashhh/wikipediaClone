Death

Andres Galdamez died in his sleep around 04:00AM on the early morning of November 2nd, 2025. Cause of death was smoke inhalation during a relatively small house fire. The cause of the fire was a giant pot of chicken that his mom left on the stovetop before going out on a night of heavy drinking and gambling.

Investigation into the pot of chicken revealed that there was plenty of water in it when his mother placed it on the stovetop. She intended to boil it for just 2 hours, but grew impatient because the alcohol and gambling addiction withdrawals were too much so she decided to leave it on the stovetop and instead blame someone else when the house filled with smoke. Because of this, the chicken was left on high heat for approximately 6 hours, which caused all the water to evaporate and leave the chicken dry, ultimately smoking it at high heat and releasing tons of smoke and polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbons into the air, which killed him. He is survived by a cute stray cat that lives in his backyard (her name is hamburger) and his 18 shares of AMD stock.
